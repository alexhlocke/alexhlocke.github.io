TweetCart Gallery

PICO-8 is a virtual "fantasy console" that allows developers to create retro-style games with limited hardware and software resources. The platform is designed to emulate the constraints of classic game consoles such as the NES, with a limited color palette, low resolution, and a small amount of memory. PICO-8 includes its own integrated development environment that allows developers to write and debug code in Lua. A big part of PICO-8's design is to encourage creativity within constraints, which can lead to a lot of interesting software and games.

Tweetcarts are a sort of challenge within the Pico-8 community to try and fit an entire program (cartridge) inside of a 280 character tweet. The program could be anything, but typically people try to make an animation or just something that looks interesting. Because of the strict character limits, you really have to get creative with condensing your code, and as a result, programs can be pretty tough to read sometimes. Nonetheless, its a fun challenge and you can do some pretty cool things with just 280 characters of LUA!

All of the tweetcarts you see here are my own!
